About me

Hello. I'm Zhining Gu. You can also call me Emily. ​ As a student in IXD program at Pratt, I’d like to focus on products and designs that related to people’s everyday life. My personal interest is on the interaction between user and products. My current goals and direction are influenced by the coops I have experienced. If you are interested in my experiences and skills, you can check my resume and portfolio at zhininggu.com.

About team

The home decoration brand EMU has two members, Emily and Helen. We want to design home decorations that can bring interesting interaction between the products and users. All our projects are handmade. Our latest project "Center piece" had all 15 sets sold during the pop-up market.
